An Introduction...

Hi, my name is Chavoughn Vassell. A little boy born in Kingston, Jamacia. I relocated to the England, UK when I was 9 years old with my family. My childhood here allowed me to build emotional and social relationships with friends whom I made. Without them, I would not have understood the feeling of feeling frustrated with the education system that have been put in place.
